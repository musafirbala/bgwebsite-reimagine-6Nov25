Digital Literacy for 10 Million Rural Women

The Challenge

Women in rural India faced significant barriers to economic participation due to lack of digital literacy, limited access to technology infrastructure, and cultural constraints on education and employment.

Our Solution

  • Technology-enabled learning platform deployed across 5,000 community centers
  • Culturally adapted curriculum in 12 regional languages
  • Peer-to-peer mentorship network
  • Mobile-first approach enabling access via feature phones
  • Financial literacy and entrepreneurship modules
